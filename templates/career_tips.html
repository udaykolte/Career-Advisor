Featured Tip of the Week

The Power of Informational Interviews

One of the most underutilized job search strategies is the informational interview. Instead of directly asking for a job, request 15-20 minutes to learn about someone's career path and industry insights. This approach builds relationships, provides valuable market intelligence, and often leads to unexpected opportunities.

  • Research and identify professionals in your target companies or roles
  • Reach out with a personalized message explaining your interest
  • Prepare thoughtful questions about their experience and industry trends
  • Follow up with a thank you note and stay in touch periodically
